How We Help You

Software Engineering

Software eats the world and it is a crucial part of the highly-interconnected European power grid. We build software products on top of the ISO MADES and CGMES standards as well as custom-tailored solutions for our customers to seamlessly integrate their business processes into the heterogeneous landscape of the European energy market, be it transmission system operators (TSOs), distribution system operators (DSOs) or energy market participants.

24/7 Operations

Building software is only half the way to production and many companies struggle with the challenges of operating complex applications in an efficient and effective way. Our expertise lies in building and operating applications for the European energy market, especially ECCo SP (ECP/EDX) and OPDM Client. We don't just build software, we know how to run it in production for 24/7 availability. Our preferred runtime stack is Linux and Kubernetes but we're also experienced in Windows and dedicated VMs.

TSO & DSO Processes

The Coppersoft team has decades of combined experience in European grid IT operations, having been part of designing today's business processes from the start and being deeply rooted in the European energy community. On the software side we employ modern technology for both, building applications and operating them efficiently. This unique combination of business and software expertise lets us build and operate applications that perfectly integrate into the European energy landscape.

Why Choose Us

The name Coppersoft represents our core skills: software engineering and operations for the energy sector. Copper symbolizes our tight relationship to the electricity industry and at the same time stands for our strengths: Among all employed materials copper provides the highest electrical conductivity and is the preferred choice for transmitting electricity. It is not the cheapest of materials but delivers the highest quality. Coppersoft is the best choice for our customers and we deliver the highest quality in the market.

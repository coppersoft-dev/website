# Coppersoft — Brand Concept

*Solid Software for Critical Infrastructure*

---

## 1. Starting Point & Ambition

Coppersoft develops **and** operates software for **critical infrastructure**. Its core lies in the energy sector — that is where its experience, track record, and deep process knowledge are rooted (TSO/DSO, ISO MADES, CGMES, ECCo SP, OPDM). But the brand does not stand for “energy IT.” It stands for the principle behind it: **software that society can rely on** — energy, water, telecommunications, transport, and everything that must not fail.

The tension that makes Coppersoft unique: **the dynamism and freshness of a startup, paired with the security and reliability that critical infrastructure demands.** Most providers in this field are one *or* the other — sluggish corporate IT or fast but unreliable startups. Coppersoft is deliberately both.

---

## 2. Brand Core

> **Coppersoft makes critical infrastructure software-capable — with the speed of a startup and the reliability of a utility provider.**

The brand rests on three pillars:

**Build.** Modern software engineering practice. Clean architecture, automation instead of manual work, standards-compliant (MADES, CGMES), and custom-tailored at the same time.

**Run.** Software is only finished once it is in production. Coppersoft operates its applications 24/7 — Linux, Kubernetes, Windows, VMs. “We don't just build software, we know how to run it.”

**Trust.** Security, reliability, and quality are not features, but the baseline requirement. In critical infrastructure, failure is not an option.

---

## 3. Positioning

**Coppersoft is the critical-infrastructure software partner that builds and operates.**

Not a pure service provider, not a pure product company, but a partner that takes responsibility for the entire lifecycle — from the first line of code to nighttime on-call duty. The energy sector is proof of its competence, not its boundary: anyone who masters the European electricity market, with its regulatory complexity and real-time requirements, can serve any critical infrastructure.

**What makes Coppersoft different:**

| | Traditional corporate IT | Generic startups | **Coppersoft** |
|---|---|---|---|
| Speed | slow | fast | **fast** |
| Reliability | high (on paper) | low | **high (in practice)** |
| Build + Run | separate | build only | **both from one source** |
| Domain knowledge | often generic | usually none | **deeply rooted** |

**Target audiences:**

- **Primary:** Operators of critical infrastructure — TSOs, DSOs, municipal utilities, utility providers; in the future also water, telecom, and transport operators.
- **Secondary:** Market participants and platforms in the European energy market that need integration and reliable operation.
- **Buying center:** IT leaders, Heads of Operations, enterprise architects — people who want to sleep soundly at night.

---

## 4. Brand Personality & Tone of Voice

Coppersoft sounds like an **excellent engineer who is also a founder**: competent, direct, free of buzzword fog, but with palpable energy and initiative.

**Values:** Reliability · Craftsmanship · Clarity · Ownership · Courage to modernize

**Tone of voice:**

- **Clear instead of inflated.** Short, precise sentences. No marketing phrases where a concrete promise is enough.
- **Confident, not arrogant.** Coppersoft proves itself through substance, not superlatives.
- **Technically grounded, explained in human terms.** Deep knowledge that even a non-technician can understand.
- **Calm under load.** The tone remains composed — fitting for someone who keeps systems running.

**Not like this:** “revolutionary,” “disruptive,” “next-gen synergy,” excessive exclamation marks.
**Like this:** “We build it. We run it. It works.”

## 5. Messaging & Claims

**Tagline (existing, sharpened):**
> **Solid Software for Critical Infrastructure**

**Alternative / complementary claims:**

- **Build it. Run it. Trust it.** — captures the three pillars in one line
- **Startup speed. Utility-grade reliability.** — the core tension as a claim
- **Software that must not fail.** — emotional, uncompromising
- **We build software for what everyone relies on.** — the breadth of critical infrastructure

**Core messages (message house):**

1. **Build + Run from one source.** Coppersoft develops software and operates it 24/7 in production — no handovers, no passing the blame, one responsibility.
2. **Deeply rooted in critical infrastructure.** Decades of combined experience, involvement in shaping today's European energy-market processes — domain knowledge that cannot simply be bought.
3. **Modern technology, uncompromising reliability.** Current engineering practice and automation, applied to systems where failure is not an option.

**Elevator pitch:**

> Coppersoft develops and operates software for critical infrastructure. We combine the speed and freshness of a modern tech team with the reliability that a utility provider needs. Our roots are in the European energy market — where software has to run in real time, around the clock, and in full regulatory compliance. We don't just build it; we keep it running.

## 6. Visual Direction

### Design Principle

**“Engineered, not decorated.”** Reduced, precise, technical — with copper as a warm, human signature. Plenty of calm space (white space / dark surfaces), clean edges, and one distinctive accent.

### Color Palette

Copper is the signature accent — but broadly legible as a symbol of **connection, conductivity, and value**, not just electricity.

| Role | Color | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Copper (primary accent)** | Copper | `#B87333` | Logo accent, CTAs, highlights |
| **Copper Bright** | Light copper | `#D89456` | Hover states, gradients, accent lines |
| **Deep Slate (dark base)** | Anthracite blue | `#0E1419` | Backgrounds, “infrastructure night” |
| **Steel** | Gray-blue | `#3A4A57` | Secondary surfaces, lines |
| **Mist (light base)** | Off-white | `#F5F3F0` | Light sections, text background |
| **Signal Green** | Clear green | `#2FA66B` | Status “running” / uptime / OK |
| **Ink** | Almost black | `#14171A` | Body text |

Logic: A dark, calm base (reliability, “always on”) + copper (warmth, connection, the namesake spark) + a signal green that visually anchors operational competence (“it's running”).

### Typography

- **Headlines:** geometric grotesque with character — e.g., **Space Grotesk** or **Sora**. Modern, slightly distinctive, “startup.”
- **Body text:** highly legible grotesque — **Inter** or **IBM Plex Sans**. Neutral, trustworthy.
- **Code / technical accents:** **IBM Plex Mono** or **JetBrains Mono** — emphasizes the engineering DNA.

Combination = the tension expressed in typography: distinctive headline character (fresh) + sober mono accent (technical, solid).

### Logo Ideas (Directions, Not Final)

1. **The copper node.** Wordmark “coppersoft,” with the “o” or a dot as a node in a network/graph — representing the connection of infrastructures. The existing circular logo can be developed further in this direction.
2. **Conductor mark.** An abstract conductor path / current path that runs through the “t” or appears as an underline — conductivity as a universal critical-infrastructure symbol.
3. **Solid dot.** A single, rich copper dot as a brand mark (heartbeat / “system online” indicator). Minimalist, scalable from favicon to trade-fair wall, and works as a status signal.

Recommendation: **wordmark in a distinctive grotesque + a reduced copper signal** (node or dot) that also works on its own as an app icon / status indicator.

### Imagery

No stock light bulbs, no generic server rooms. Instead: **macro textures of copper and material, real infrastructure in a calm aesthetic, abstract network/graph visualizations, dark UI screenshots with a copper accent.** Honest, technical, premium.
